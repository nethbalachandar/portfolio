Deployment Strategy

This model can be integrated into a fab’s manufacturing execution system (MES) or SPC dashboard to monitor sensor values in real time. By flagging wafers at risk of failure before inspection, it enables engineers to take preventive action, adjust process parameters, and reduce scrap. Regular retraining with new wafer lots ensures ongoing accuracy.
